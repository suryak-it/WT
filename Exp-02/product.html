New - EyeTrends

Eyeglass frames come in various styles,
materials, and designs to suit different preferences and face shapes.
Common frame materials include plastic, metal,
titanium, and wood.

One of the primary features of computer glasses
is their ability to filter out or reduce blue light emitted by digital screens.
Blue light is a high-energy, short-wavelength light that is emitted by
digital devices and artificial lighting.

Cat eye glasses are a style of eyeglasses characterized
by their upswept outer edges, which resemble the shape of a cat's eye.
This iconic design has been popular since the mid-20th century and is often
associated with vintage and retro fashion

Rim glasses,
also known as full-rim glasses,
are a type of eyeglasses where the lenses are
completely surrounded by a frame
