EDITH

Edith, short for "Even Dead I'm The Hero,"
Is a fictional artificial intelligence (AI) augmented reality glasses in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).It stands out prominently in the film "Spider-Man: Far From Home" (2019).Edith is created by Tony Stark,the billionaire genius and former CEO of Stark Industries. After Stark's death in "Avengers: Endgame," his legacy lives on through Edith,which is entrusted to Peter Parker, also known as Spider-Man. Edith grants Peter access to Stark's extensive resources and technology, allowing him to continue the fight against villains and threats to humanity.
